About Us

Team IJKL met in a dark alley one evening, each having acquired special skills from their respective lurid backgrounds. After a fearsome battle in which each pulled their weight (which stands at a combined eighty-eight pounds) and much blood was shed, the team found out that they were in fact all blood-related. Realising their strength in numbers, the team formed a contract that prevents any member from being more than five metres away from any other member for more than thirty seconds. This contract also forbids each member from getting married; having children; meeting new people or seeing their friends or families. A daunting contract indeed when you consider the combined age of the members is five hundred and sixty years.
