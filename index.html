About Us

We met in a dark alley one evening, each having acquired special skills from their respective lurid backgrounds.

After a fearsome battle in which each pulled their weight (which stands at a combined eighty-eight pounds) and much blood was shed, the team found out that they were in fact all blood-related.

Realising their strength in numbers, the team formed a contract that prevents any member from being more than five metres away from any other member for more than thirty seconds.

This contract also forbids each member from getting married; having children; meeting new people or seeing their friends or families.

A daunting contract indeed when you consider the combined age of the members is five hundred and sixty years.

Christine

In the deepest, darkest jungles of Peru, Christine met her mentor and lifelong fashion icon, Paddington Bear. With fists of fury and a level temperament, Christine can be messed with but shan't allow it to bother her!

Colette

Born from the ashes of a fallen phoenix, Colette began life disappointed that she was not born a phoenix. Rising on a path of fire of her own, at a young age she mastered the disciplines of vegetable spiralising and robot building.

Reuben

Reuben, 6, has been coding for two decades. He is often surprised when his code runs, as it is only valid by coincidence and is actually all a failed attempt to write in French. Reuben actually hates the nickname "Reuben," and would prefer to be called by his birth name, "Reuuuuuubennnnnn."

Gregor

Gregor was born in London in 1989. He took ten years of full-time speech therapy to acquire his distinctive Scottish accent, after which he found out that this kind of education was not enough to get a job. This is when he turned to coding.
